Apex Legends Global Series

What is ALGS?

The Apex Legends Global Series (ALGS) is the official competitive circuit for Apex Legends, organized by Electronic Arts and Respawn Entertainment. It brings together the world's most elite squads in an epic test of skill, communication, and composure.

ALGS Championship Overview

The ALGS Championship is the pinnacle of professional Apex Legends—a global event where only the strongest, smartest, and most synchronized teams stand a chance. After grinding through online qualifiers and intense LAN matches, the top 40 teams in the world converge to battle it out in a high-stakes, high-pressure tournament.

Record-Breaking Viewership

Every ALGS event raises the bar, drawing in massive viewership numbers across Twitch, YouTube, and other platforms. The Championship especially sees Apex Legends fans from all over the globe tuning in to witness the most intense moments of the season.
